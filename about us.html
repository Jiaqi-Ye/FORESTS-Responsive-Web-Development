About Us

How Do We Operate?

We sustain the operation of the Forest website through creative product sales and economic support from partner organizations. All funds raised are placed into the organization’s reserve to support daily operations and respond to emergencies. We promise that Forest does not retain profits—every dollar raised is donated to national environmental initiatives and forest conservation efforts. For every 100 RMB spent on Forest, we plant one tree.

      Learn About Our Work

We promote environmental awareness, share updates on forest conditions, help locate nearby recycling stations, collaborate with artisans and charities, and provide a community and marketplace for eco-friendly products.

Our Mission

The 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) guide global policies toward a sustainable and resilient future by 2030. Sustainability is at the core of our environmental initiatives. Forest contributes to several SDGs, including environmental education and forest ecosystem protection.
