About Forest

Follow Forest is a public platform focused on forest ecology and environmental culture. Here you can learn about forest conditions and environmental knowledge, and purchase creative products to make a positive impact on forests worldwide. All platform profits support global environmental causes.

May We Not Be the Last Children of the Forest

Global forests face an unprecedented crisis. Every two minutes, an area the size of a soccer field disappears. Deforestation and degradation are caused by many factors, and human negligence toward environmental protection and recycling is a major one.

Our Impact

Forest was founded on one key idea: knowledge is power. Only by understanding environmental knowledge can we help solve forest ecosystem issues. By learning and taking action—whether recycling or purchasing creative products—you advocate change and support our mission for global sustainability.
